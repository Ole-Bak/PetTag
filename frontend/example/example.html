1. Get a Tag

Our Story

PetTag was born out of a personal scare. In 2023, our founder's dog, Barnaby, slipped his leash in a new city. While he had a traditional engraved tag, the phone number was old.

It took hours to find him through local shelters. We realized there had to be a better, dynamic way to manage pet ID. Thus, PetTag was created — combining the durability of a physical tag with the flexibility of the cloud.
